"source": "Texas State University housing policy",
"content": "Texas State requires new freshmen under 20 years old with less than 30 semester credit hours to live on-campus. However, students who graduated from high school within a year of their semester of admission are required to live on-campus regardless of earned semester credit hours. Students may only file for an exemption if they are married, have or are expecting children, have a disability that the university cannot accommodate or can commute from a parent’s house within a 50-mile driving distance of campus. For context, Texas Tech University has a similar policy, while the University of Texas at Austin does not have an on-campus living requirement."
